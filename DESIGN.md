# Project Design Document: Campus Compass

This document outlines the design, key features, and development plan for the "Campus Compass" project.

## Project Overview

The "Campus Compass" is a web application designed to simplify the transition for new international and national students arriving in Hildesheim, Germany. It will serve as a hybrid tool, offering both a pre-populated, comprehensive guide to essential university and city-related tasks, and a personal to-do list where students can manage their own unique tasks.

**Our Goal:** To empower new students to confidently navigate their initial challenges in Hildesheim, fostering a sense of belonging and ensuring they can fully embrace the "land of plenty" that Hildesheim offers. The platform aims for **information dissipation** in both **German and English**.

**Target Audience:** All students at Uni Hildesheim, including those pursuing **Bachelors, Masters, PhD, Post-Graduate, and Professors**.

**Key Principles:**

* **Simplicity:** Easy-to-use and intuitive interface.

* **Personalization:** Ability for individual students to track their progress and add custom tasks.

* **Compliance:** Adherence to EU regulations on Privacy and Policy.

* **Compatibility:** Fully functional and optimized for **Phone, Tablet, and PC**.

## Proposed Features

### Part 1: Landing Page

This section will be the public-facing entry point for all students, providing essential information and access points.

* **FAQs:** A dedicated section for Frequently Asked Questions relevant to new students.
* **About Us:** Information detailing the project's purpose and introducing the team (Adi & Uttam).
* **Contact Us:** A clear method for users to get in touch, likely a form or contact details.
* **Handbook Download:** A feature allowing users to download a comprehensive student handbook or onboarding kit.
* **Navigation Bar:** A standard navigation menu at the top of the page for easy site traversal.
* **Login Access:** Prominent login and registration links, likely located in the navigation bar.

### Part 2: Dashboard (Logged-in Experience)

This section will be accessible only after a user logs in, offering personalized tools and interactive features.

* **Interactive To-Do List:** A comprehensive personal task management system, including:
    * Adding, editing, deleting, and marking tasks as complete.
    * Support for **tags** to categorize tasks.
    * Simple management nuances for intuitive use.
* **Path Way:** A visual or guided feature illustrating the complete student journey, from initial arrival to full integration.
* **Interactive Maps:** Dynamic maps that function as a directory, enabling easy navigation within the university campus and surrounding areas.
* **Train and Plane Routes:** Information showcasing major cities and providing clear guidance on how to travel to Hildesheim from these locations.
